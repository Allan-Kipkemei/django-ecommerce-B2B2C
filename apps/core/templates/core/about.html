About Sokopark

Sokopark is an online marketplace that provides a wide range of products with focus on variety, fast delivery and competitive prices. We strive to be the go-to platform for the different needs of Rwandan consumers. We also aim to assist our clients in meeting their projects requirements at all stages.

With emphasis on reliability and respect of high quality, we continue to nurture solid and strategic relationships with a network of exclusively-selected manufacturers and vendors in an effort to provide optimal solutions to fit the specific demands of our clients.
