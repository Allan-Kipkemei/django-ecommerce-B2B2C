Vendor Guidelines

Account Requirements

  • Full registration certificate (RDB).
  • Momo merchant code.
  • Bank account details.
  • High resolution company logo.

    • Product Management

  • The vendor must provide detailed & accurate information.
  • It is the responsibility of the vendor to update the products information (stock, delivery times).
  • The vendor should not attempt to increase the price of a product after an order is confirmed.
  • Images : must be in high definition and the main image must have a white background.

    • Customer Information

  • You may receive customer information such as addresses or phone numbers. You can only use that information to fulfill orders. You may not share it with any third-party.
